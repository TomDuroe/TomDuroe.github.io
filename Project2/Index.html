My Games

Call of Duty Warzone

Warzone is the newest contender to the battle royal genre but already it has become the best. Its shooting is satisfying, its map rewarding to explore, and it offers more than enough interesting concepts like the Gulag and Contracts to make each match feel wholly unique, wherever you land.

Apex Legends

Apex Legends is another game which has delved into the battle royale genre. Featuring intuitive controls, an outstanding comms system and remarkable rogue’s gallery, Apex is probably the most fun youll have in this style of game.

Destiny 2

Similar to its predecessor, Destiny 2 is a first-person shooter game that incorporates role-playing and massively multiplayer online game elements. Destiny 2 allows you to delve into the epic loot systems, character classes and a truly operatic sci-fi universe to become the most powerful guardian.

Bloodborne

Bloodborne is an action rpg set in the Victorian era–inspired city of Yharnam, whose inhabitants are afflicted with a blood-borne disease. Attempting to find the source of the plague, the player's character unravels the city's mysteries while fighting beasts and cosmic beings.
